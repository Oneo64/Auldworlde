Moons:
	An,  yellow moon (looks like Io) (English name is Aan)
		An represents fire, sulfur, and heat.
		In Oddarsveldi, An is called "Ána."

	Lûn, white moon
		Lûn represents snow, the cold, and silver.
		In Oddarsveldi, Lûn is called "Ljúna."

	Vel, blue moon
		Vel represents water, the ocean, and magic.
		In Oddarsveldi, Lûn is called "Vél."

	Once every few decades, An rubs past on Lûn's surface, causing a bright flash and some small meteors.
