Kolr

Male
Age: Early 30s
Alignment: Neutral

Homeland:  Álahringr, Fjalland
Race:      Human



[[DESCRIPTION]]
Kolr is a blacksmith who works in Álahringr. He is described as having brown hair, and a full beard.

He wears a grey tunic, grey pants, shoes, and a ring belt.



[[PERSONALITY]]
Kolr is described as a welcoming person. He usually asks a lot of questions before doing a commission.



[[ABILITIES]]
Kolr is able to make axes, swords, spears, helmets, and other things. He doesn't know how to make chainmail.
