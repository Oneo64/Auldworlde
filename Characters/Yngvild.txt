[[DESCRIPTION]]
Yngvild has dark blond and wavy hair, which reaches to her armpits. Her eyes are dark pale blue, and has fair skin. She wears a long yellow tunic down to her ankles, a ring belt, and a pair of shoes. She has a small seax sheathed on her belt. She wears an iron Talmundúr amulet. She is 163 (5'4) centimeters tall.

Yngvild and Meyrúna are friends from childhood.



[[PERSONALITY]]
Yngvild is generally optimistic, and can be playful at times. She also has a slight paranoia when around centaurs, worried they might be hostile.



[[ABILITIES]]
The only language Yngvild can speak and understand is Fjallandic, her native language. Yngvild can also play the talharpa well.
