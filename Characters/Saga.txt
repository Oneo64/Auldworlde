Sága

Female
Age: 20
Alignment: Neutral

Homeland:  Álahringr, Fjalland
Race:      Human

Father:    Hjalvir



[[DESCRIPTION]]
Sága has long black hair, pale green eyes, and has fair skin.

She wears a dark cloak, a dark red tunic, dark pants, a ring belt, a pair of shoes, and an iron Talmundúr amulet. She also wears a nasal helmet in combat.



[[PERSONALITY]]
Sága, like her father, is adventurous and curious. She is also talkative, but isn't overly extroverted.



[[ABILITIES]]
Sága speaks Fjallandic as her native language. Sága sports a sword and shield, but isn't very good at it.
