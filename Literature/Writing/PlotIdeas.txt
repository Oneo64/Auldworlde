Ljörn is pressured by his brother Alfrir to join him on an expedition to a new land. After arriving at the new land, the group must try to establish a farmstead without succumbing to the various monsters there.

They arrive at the land. A few hours after arrival, Hjalvir arranges an exploring team, who Ljörn, Alfrir, Ingólfr, Rúna, and Sága is set on. Ljörn and Alfrir were chosen because of their experience with supernatural creatures, Ingólfr is to lead, Rúna to identify herbs, and Sága because she's Hjalvir's daughter.
